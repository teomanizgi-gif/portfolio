SpeedSense Smart Planter Mycelium Project Turbine Kit

Problem

Conventional wall coverings such as ceramic tiles and wallpaper often come with environmental, functional, and aesthetic limitations. Their production processes can be resource-intensive, while installation methods are typically permanent and can damage existing wall surfaces.

In addition, these materials offer limited flexibility — once applied, they are difficult to modify or replace.

Mycelium Blocks are designed as a natural, aesthetically refined, and sustainable alternative to conventional wall coverings such as ceramic tiles and wallpaper. The system rethinks interior surfaces by introducing a bio-based material that integrates both function and environmental responsibility.
The modular block system is arranged in rows, forming an organic, textured surface that breaks from the rigid repetition of conventional wall finishes. This approach introduces warmth and depth while maintaining a refined, contemporary expression. Beyond its visual qualities, mycelium offers long-term durability, exceeding 15 years in lifespan compared to standard wallpapers. Functioning as both a wall covering and a tile, it seamlessly merges decorative surface with structural performance.
