SpeedSense Smart Planter Mycelium Project Turbine Kit

Problem

Beginner skiers often struggle to control their speed and lack awareness of how fast they are going. Without enough experience or physical control, they can easily exceed safe speed limits, increasing the risk of falls and injuries.

Even when instructors are present, real-time awareness of speed is limited. In many cases, beginners can’t recognize when they’re approaching dangerous speeds, which makes them more vulnerable on the slopes.

    Solution

    SpeedSense is a wearable wristband designed to help beginner skiers monitor and control their speed in real time. By using haptic feedback instead of visual or auditory alerts, the device allows users to stay focused on their movement and surroundings while receiving immediate physical feedback about their speed.

    How It Works

    - At 25 mph, the wristband activates intermittent vibration (pulse mode), warning the user that they are approaching a risky speed.

    - At 30 mph and above, the device switches to continuous vibration, signaling that the speed limit has been exceeded.

    - The vibration continues until the user slows down to a safer level.

The product is designed as a lightweight and comfortable wristband, allowing freedom of movement while skiing. Its minimal form ensures that it does not interfere with performance, while remaining easily noticeable through tactile feedback.
SpeedSense transforms speed awareness into a physical experience, enabling beginner skiers to react instantly without distraction. By providing real-time feedback, it helps reduce accidents, improve confidence, and support a safer learning process on the slopes.
