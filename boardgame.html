SpeedSense Smart Planter Mycelium Project Turbine Kit

Problem

In remote and rugged areas of the UK, such as national parks and campsites, phone signal coverage is often limited or nonexistent. This creates serious challenges for travellers, hikers, and outdoor enthusiasts, especially in emergencies where communication becomes critical.

In addition, the lack of reliable power sources makes it difficult to keep essential electronic devices charged. As a result, users face both communication and energy limitations, increasing safety risks in outdoor environments.

This project introduces a GSM communication and power bank kit powered by wind energy, designed specifically for off-grid outdoor use.

By harnessing wind as a renewable energy source, the system enables users to generate power independently, eliminating reliance on traditional charging methods. The integrated GSM functionality allows users to stay connected even in low-signal environments, supporting both emergency communication and everyday use.

Features

  • Power Bank (10,000 mAh)
  • GSM Device
  • GSM Device Kit
  • USB Port
  • Wind Turbine Structure
