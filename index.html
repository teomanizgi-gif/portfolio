About

I am a furniture and product designer based in London, graduated from London South Bank University in 2024 with a degree in Product Design. My work is driven by a minimal, refined approach, often leaning towards understated luxury, while always remaining grounded in practicality and real-world production experience.

Designing products is not just a profession for me; it is a passion and an artistic expression. Each design process becomes a unique journey — a way of telling a new story through form, material, and function. I constantly seek the balance where creativity meets functionality, and where aesthetics seamlessly integrate with user experience.

Alongside my design practice, I have hands-on experience in physical manufacturing, particularly in wood workshops, where I have worked with CNC and laser-cutting machines to bring ideas to life. I am also interested in working with sustainable materials, exploring ways to create more responsible and long-lasting products. I have a basic understanding of digital design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite and Figma, supporting my broader approach to user experience.

My goal is to design products that simplify, support, and elevate daily living, while maintaining a strong visual identity and a clear sense of purpose.
