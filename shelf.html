TESU Project

TESU is a modular metal wall system designed for flexibility and adaptability. Its perforated surface allows shelves and storage units to be easily arranged and repositioned in various configurations, enabling users to customise the system according to their needs.
Additional storage components can be integrated over time, offering scalability and long-term usability. At the same time, the system encourages a balance between function and composition — leaving certain areas intentionally open to create visual rhythm and spatial clarity.
TESU transforms storage into a dynamic, evolving surface where utility and aesthetic composition coexist.

Tree-Inspired Wall Shelf

This wall-mounted shelf is inspired by the organic structure of a tree, with a central vertical element serving as the trunk and horizontal shelves extending like branches. The design translates a natural growth logic into a functional storage system. The asymmetrical arrangement of the shelves creates a dynamic composition, allowing objects to be placed in a visually balanced yet non-uniform way.
The entire piece is crafted from oak wood, enhancing the natural warmth and authenticity of the design. The subtle grain texture and light tone of oak contribute to a clean, contemporary aesthetic while also reinforcing the connection to nature.

Illuminated Oak Shelf

This shelf is designed as a combination of storage and integrated lighting, creating both a functional and atmospheric element within the space. The form is defined by continuous lines and soft transitions, resulting in a clean yet expressive architectural silhouette.
The entire structure is crafted from oak wood, bringing warmth, natural texture, and a refined visual quality to the design. An integrated lighting system is embedded in the frame, emitting a soft, diffused glow.
Rather than functioning as a conventional storage unit, the design merges furniture and lighting into a single composition.

Illuminated Storage Unit

This shelving unit is designed as a multifunctional piece that combines storage, display, and lighting within a single architectural form. The recessed structure creates a framed niche, allowing objects to be presented in a defined and visually focused composition.
The outer body is crafted from wood, bringing warmth and a natural character to the design, while the darker inner shelves create contrast and depth. An integrated lighting element within the niche enhances the displayed objects and establishes a soft, ambient atmosphere.
In addition to open shelving, the unit includes a lower drawer compartment for concealed storage of everyday items.
